Who we are

Magic Billion Global Skills Academy

Magic Billion Global Skill Academy is an education and career mobility company helping young Indians build international careers. We combine German language training, globally recognised degrees, and end-to-end placement support to take talented people from India to Germany and across Europe.

About Magic Billion

Our Story

For nearly 8 years, Magic Billion has specialised in skilling and placing young Indians in global careers. What began as a mission to connect Indian talent with international opportunity has grown into a full ecosystem of language training, accredited education, and career placement, trusted by thousands of students and families.

About India Works

Support Abroad

IndiaWorks is our career readiness and placement arm. We have supported 1,000+ candidates into Germany-linked careers, guiding them through professional integration, interview preparation, and the move abroad. From your rst class to your rst day at work overseas, IndiaWorks is with you.

Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To democratize access to elite international employment and academic pathways by eliminating traditional financial bottlenecks, high debt risks and complex immigration friction for Indian youth.

Our Vision

To serve as the most trusted, ethical and government-approved talent infrastructure bridge connecting Indian ambitions directly with Europe’s largest corporate economies.

Our Values

Built on absolute regulatory compliance, financial transparency, academic rigor and an unyielding commitment to lifelong candidate career integration.
